# Hulkbuster Helmet

## Design Specifications

### Overview
Hulkbuster massive heavy industrial helmet. Features extreme proportions, heavy armor plating, and industrial yellow/red color scheme.

### Key Design Elements

**Massive Cylindrical Profile:**
- 125% scale (much larger)
- Angular superellipsoid (n=1.7-1.8)
- Heavy top plate and armor ridges
- Side armor plates with bolts
- Rear exhaust ports

**Heavy Faceplate:**
- Massive brow ridge
- Heavy cheek guards (primary and secondary)
- Center reinforcement
- Industrial styling

**Industrial Jaw:**
- Massive chin guard
- Side reinforcements
- Hydraulic cylinders
- Bottom vent

**Massive Ear Pieces:**
- Large housing (30mm radius)
- Outer ring detail
- Heavy mount plate
- Six large bolts
- Rear exhaust

**Technical Specs:**
- 125% scale
- Thicker walls (4.0mm)
- Higher resolution (64 segments)
- Target triangles: 30,000-50,000
- Industrial yellow/red color scheme

### 3D Printing Notes
- Very large print (125% scale)
- Thick walls require more material
- Heavy supports needed for overhangs
- Consider splitting into parts for printing
- 0.2mm layer height acceptable
- High infill (25-30%) for strength
