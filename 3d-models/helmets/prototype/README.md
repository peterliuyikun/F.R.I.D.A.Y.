# Prototype Helmet

## Design Specifications

### Overview
Prototype helmet with raw mechanical design and exposed hydraulics. Features industrial aesthetic with visible mechanics and unfinished look.

### Key Design Elements

**Mechanical Cylindrical Profile:**
- Exposed frame struts (8 radial)
- Top hydraulic cylinder
- Bolt heads around circumference
- Thicker walls (3.25mm)

**Exposed Faceplate:**
- Hydraulic pistons on sides
- Piston housings
- Center hydraulic cylinder
- Exposed wiring channels

**Hydraulic Jaw:**
- Side hydraulic cylinders
- Mount brackets
- Chin hydraulic
- Exposed bolts

**Mechanical Ear Pieces:**
- Octagonal housing
- Exposed "gear" details
- Hydraulic lines
- Mount bolts

**Technical Specs:**
- Industrial aesthetic
- Many exposed mechanical details
- Target triangles: 25,000-40,000
- Scale: 102% of life-size
- Raw metal/unfinished color scheme

### 3D Printing Notes
- Many small details require fine resolution
- Supports needed for struts and hydraulics
- Consider metallic filament
- 0.1-0.15mm layer height for details
